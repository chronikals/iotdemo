Introduction

Why a common look and feel?

“Harmonize and improve the user experience of ABB products to enable increased production, safety and reliability for our customers”

Our goals

  • Create a movement - successfully initiate UX harmonization effort across ABB

  • Develop an easy-to-use UX style guide to help product teams deliver consistent and improved user experiences

  • Harmonize the look & feel of the ABB software portfolio to increase attractiveness and thereby increase product sales.

Our vision

Software interfaces in ABB

ABB has a large variety of different software user interfaces and the main challenge is to find a common look and feel that works on all formats. Beside various screens there are contrary work situations, for example office vs field. also, there are challenges related to different user interaction methods like touch, keyboard and mouse, trackpads, etc.
